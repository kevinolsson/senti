Who we are

The social media revolution and the way we communicate have caused inevitable change to the general paradigm of advertising and promotions. The key to thriving is ensuring your awareness of what your market says. The hyper-evolution of language has pushed us to formulate programs that take these elements and venues and use these for data-gathering and promotion of the highest efficiency. SENTi and our platforms move with the times to provide feedback from your target demographics as these factions utilize an ever-shifting language game ordinary analytics tools are unable to process.

SENTi develops cutting-edge monitoring and analysis algorithms that assist you in online presence optimization through the in-depth analysis of feedback from social media. We are the only platform that allows you to put your finger on pulse of your market via sophisticated analytical tools. These tools understand keywords and terms in English, Filipino, and the vernacular in its many variations. These terms can make or break your data-gathering and influence the success of your online campaigns.

SENTi’s proprietary tools filter what’s on the hive mind to determine how and what your audience thinks. With this information, you can act and react effectively to bolster your bottom line. Keep your business in the loop, your presence in the zone, and your products and services in the minds of your audience. We work with you towards a firm foothold in a hostile market, and better shot at making it to the top. We give you access to emergent data that energizes your insights, fortifies your efforts, and boosts your chances at exponential growth and business success.
